Why we do it

1 in 8 households in Canada is food insecure.

We provide students with complimentary lunch because students shouldn't learn on an empty stomach.

70% of our team are women or POC.

Diversity doesn't happen by accident. Our team reflects the students and communities we serve.

1 in 4 parents can't afford school supplies.

Not everyone can afford the latest school supplies or a laptop, which is why we provide students with free tools and resources.
